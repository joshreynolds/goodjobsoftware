About

March 25, 2026

I’m a technology leader with a lifelong passion for building things. It started when my dad brought home an IBM 386 — I was seven years old, and I’ve been hooked ever since. What began as curiosity about how computers work grew into a career spanning the full arc of the modern internet.

Background#

Over the past few decades I’ve worked across startups, mid-size companies, and large organizations, wearing nearly every hat along the way — developer, architect, team lead, and executive. I’m currently CTO at a sport ticketing platform, where I oversee the technology strategy, engineering team, and product infrastructure.

I’ve built systems from bare metal up, navigated legacy codebases, scaled platforms under pressure, and helped teams ship software they’re proud of. My experience runs deep across cloud infrastructure, full-stack development, DevOps, and AI — but what I find most rewarding is building the people and processes that make great technology possible.

What I’m Looking For#

I’m actively exploring new opportunities in technology leadership — CTO, VP of Engineering, or similar roles where I can help an organization grow, modernize, or solve hard problems at scale. I thrive in environments that value ownership, pragmatism, and craftsmanship.

If you’re building something interesting and need a leader who can go deep technically while keeping the bigger picture in focus, I’d love to connect.

Outside of Work#

When I’m not in front of a screen you’ll find me outdoors — camping, hiking, and boating when the weather cooperates, and throwing darts at the local Lions Club when it doesn’t.
